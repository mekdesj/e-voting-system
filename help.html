How to Use the Voting System

1. Register an Account

To participate in the voting process, you must create an account. Follow these steps:

  • Click on the Register button on the Home page.
  • Fill out the registration form with your name, student ID, and other required information.
  • Set a secure password for your account.
  • Submit the form to complete the registration process.

2. Log In to Your Account

After registration, follow these steps to log in:

  • Click on the Login button on the Home page.
  • Enter your registered Student ID and password.
  • Click Login to access your voting dashboard.

3. Cast Your Vote

Once you have logged in, follow these steps to cast your vote:

  • Browse the list of candidates displayed on the voting dashboard.
  • Select the candidate you wish to vote for by clicking the Vote button next to their name.
  • Confirm your choice when prompted.

4. Logout

For security reasons, always log out after voting. Click the Logout button at the top of the page.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here are some commonly asked questions to help you:

  • What if I forget my password? Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it.
  • Can I change my vote after submission? No, once submitted, your vote is final.
  • Who do I contact if I face technical issues? Contact the Student Union IT Support Team using the Contact Page.
